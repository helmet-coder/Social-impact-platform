🔧 Right to Repair

Why It Matters

Modern devices are intentionally hard to fix, leading to more waste, less ownership, and expensive replacements. Right to Repair is about making tools, parts, and documentation available to everyone.

  • 🗑️ Reduces global e-waste (over 50 million tons yearly)
  • 💸 Saves consumers money and increases product life
  • 🌱 Encourages sustainability and local repair jobs
